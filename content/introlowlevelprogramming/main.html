This document will teach you the basics of how computers and simmilar devices work at its (2nd) deepest level. (The first being the literal wires and circuitry)

Depending on which generation of electronic devices you are working with, low-level programming can change a lot, so this document only teaches the things that apply to most, if not all devices.

I am also more used to earlier generation devices, such as the SNES, so there may be a few things that I miss from modern devices. (Such as virtual addressing, that one's painfull to think about)
